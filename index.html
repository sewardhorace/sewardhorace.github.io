Dungeon World Online (Work in Progress)

Dungeon World is originally a tabletop RPG by Adam Koebel and Sage LaTorra. I'm making a web app to play it with friends online. Originally started as a Rails app, I'm now moving into using Meteor.

This project has been a ton of fun and a huge learning experience. I've used Twitter Bootstrap, jQuery, React, HTML5 Canvas, and deployed with Heroku.

SquishyPurpleRutabaga

A silly iOS app I made for my sister's birthday. I asked her for three words to inspire an idea - guess what they were.

The rutabaga searches brainyquote.com with the user's text input and adds some randomization after parsing the HTML results. Then the creepy voice speaks to you (using a simple text to speech API). Written in Objective-C.
