Dungeon World Online (WIP)

Dungeon World is originally a tabletop RPG by Adam Koebel and Sage LaTorra. This is a Rails app that allows groups to play online at their leisure, rather than having to get together for 3-5 hours at a time. The basic skeleton of the site is in place, and I'm now working on integrating the actual gameplay mechanics.

I used Twitter Bootstrap to make the site fully mobile-responsive and have been working with jQuery UI for gameplay-related interactions.

SquishyPurpleRutabaga

A silly iOS app I made for my sister's birthday. I asked her for three words to inspire an idea; Guess what they were.

The rutabaga searches brainyquote.com with the user's text input and adds some randomization after parsing the HTML results. Then the creepy voice speaks to you (using a simple text to speech API). Written in Objective-C.
